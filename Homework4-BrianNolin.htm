Background

Escapes rooms are trending in 2018, but still on the rise! Escape rooms feature obstacles and puzzles that users must overcome through clues, problem-solving, and strategy.

Escape Room Games

Escape Room Games is a mobile game available on Android devices. Escape Room Games consists of levels with vastly different themes where the protagonist must assess and conquer the situation in a timely manner to stay alive.

The goal of Escape Room Games is to combine the nostalgia of old escape room video games with the challenge of modern escape rooms.
